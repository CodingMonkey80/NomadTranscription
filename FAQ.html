What file formats do you accept?

We accept audio and video recordings in MP3, MP4, WMV, AIF, M4A, MOV, AVI, VOB, AMR, WMA, OGG, AAC, and WAV. We do not currently accept physical media (tapes, CDs, SD cards, etc.) If you have a file in a format other than those listed here, please Contact Us. We also accept links to streaming video and audio files available on the web.

Do you identify speakers?

Yes - we identify each individual speaker and keep the reference consistent throughout the transcript. If the recording has more than three speakers, speakers may be referenced as Male or Female rather than individually numbered.
